# Chapter Five – Mass Deportation

## Dooney’s Deportation Plan 2023

1. 
Dooney’s Deportation Plan 2023,
Are as absurd as herding cats, you see.
Bobby, our hero, spent his days,
Searching for needles in haystack maze.
He loved the smell of hay at dawn,
With counting robots buzzing like bees on the lawn.

2.
 Every first Thursday, Dooney would shout,
With a bullhorn, “Undocumented, come out!”
He posted signs in fields of corn,
“Stand here if you’re undocumented, forlorn.”

3.
 How does he plan to find these folks?
It’s laughable, just like bad jokes.
They come from all over, not just one place,
Old men, young women, kids with toys in the race.
Dooney’s clueless, can’t identify,
Who’s who, no matter how hard he’ll try.

4.
Employers keep secrets, tight as a drum,
Twenty works, but only three to the IRS come.
Like his first run, when he claimed with glee,
Mexico would pay for the wall, you see.
Mexico promised, when they win the Lotto,
It’s all so unrealistic, just bravado.

5.
Dooney’s into selling grand ideas,
But making them happen? That’s not his sphere.
Logistics? That’s someone else’s chore,
He’s more focused on selling dreams galore.
