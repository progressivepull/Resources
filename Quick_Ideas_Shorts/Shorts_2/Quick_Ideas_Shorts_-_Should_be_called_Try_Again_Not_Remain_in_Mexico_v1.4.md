# Chapter Two – Should be Called Try Again Not Remain in Mexico     


## Try Again       

1.  
Trump implemented a policy often referred to as " Remain in Mexico," but should be called "Try Again". When undocumented immigrants cross the border, they are sent back to Mexico, essentially giving them another chance to re-enter the United States.    

2.
With this policy in place, over 70% of those who cross the border illegally still manage to make it through. President Biden recognized the issues caused by this policy and attempted to eliminate it.    

3.
However, the court system intervened and ruled that the policy must remain in place. People should not have to risk their lives to become American citizens.
